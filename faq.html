FAQ

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
    Select the following printer for your job: HZ Printer. When the print job is given, log in using the HZ pass or by entering your HZ username & password. Then choose the Print Release option. You will then see which print jobs are ready. If there is sufficient balance in your print account, the print jobs will be printed. Tap Access Device to see your balance.
  2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
    Log on to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your HZ user name & password. Choose the Scan option in the menu. Place the original(s) on the feeder or glass plate. Double-sided scanning (optional) Press Change settings For duplex mode, select the 2-sided option. Press Start
  3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?
    Go to Hz shop page and buy what you need.
  4. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
    Check the option type of meeting room to see all meeting rooms that are available for booking. These meeting rooms are equipped with additional AV resources such as a webcam. This makes it possible to meet online or give lessons here. The square behind the room number indicates how many people can use this room. After completing the application, the room is immediately reserved for you.
